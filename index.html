Help & support

Support

Thanks for playing Sports Agent. If you need help or want to report an issue, we're here.

How do I play?

Tap a player to sign them. Watch their value rise and sell before they peak. You have 200 weeks — roughly two minutes — to make as much money as possible.

What do the symbols mean?

  • ⭐ STAR? — This player is likely to rise significantly. Sign early and hold your nerve.
  • ⚠️ TROUBLE? — High risk. Could boom, could bust. Mostly dangerous.
  • 🎲 GAMBLE! — Genuinely 50/50. Could recover a falling player or accelerate the drop.

How is my score ranked?

Your profit at the end of each game adds to your career total and earns you an Agent Rank — from Bootroom all the way up to Transfer Window Legend.

Can I change sport?

Yes — tap the menu button (☰) during a game and select Football or Rugby.

How do I turn sound off?

Tap the menu button (☰) during a game. Sound On/Off is in that menu.

The app crashed / something isn't working

Please email us with a description of what happened and what device you're using. We'll get back to you as quickly as we can.
